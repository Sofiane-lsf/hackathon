Our solution



Elephant is the room has developed a low tech application for women farmers in Botswana. The application allow them to create a community and help each other to prevent the Elephants destroying their fields and therefore guarantee their harvest.
We enliven those informations with meteorological data and we produce analysis regarding the movements, the habits and the needs of the Elephants

Our created value

Knowing that the tourism industry is a fast-growing economy for Botswana - and in the other hand that the killing of Elephants and wilderness will slow this industry - we want to help tourism companies to improve their business, support the local economy and play a role in the preservation of the country's wilderness

Here are the benefits for our clients :

  • Add new value to your Safaris offers
  • Create unique experiences for your customers
  • Differentiate from your competitors
